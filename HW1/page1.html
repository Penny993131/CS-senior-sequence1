Welcome to Yantai

The sleepy portside town of Yantai (烟台, Yāntái) has managed to court foreign investment in its high-tech industry while building itself into a popular beach resort with a distinctive treaty port history. A tunnel connects the older, tourist-friendly district of Zhifu (芝罘, Zhīfú) with the booming Laishan (莱山, Láishān) district to the southeast. For now, this is still a place where you can take things easy. And with Penglai Pavilion not far away, the town makes for a relaxing two-day sojourn.
